01 — Who We Are

A mature infrastructure operator for a volatile energy market.

Evolta Energy is a Haiti-based energy solutions company built on over two decades of industry experience and a proven track record of energy system installations across the country. Our leadership has been directly involved in the engineering, deployment, and operational management of energy infrastructure in complex and demanding environments.

Haiti's energy landscape is defined by unstable grid supply, heavy reliance on diesel generation, and volatile fuel pricing. Our clients are not simply buying solar panels — they are seeking predictable energy costs, reduced fuel dependency, and a trusted long-term partner.

Evolta was founded to provide reliable, engineered, and service-driven solutions in a market where long-term accountability and execution quality are too often inconsistent. We compete on reliability, predictability, and total cost of energy.

Lifecycle Service

Monitoring, preventative maintenance, and long-term performance oversight.

Beyond turnkey systems, Evolta selectively supplies key components — inverters, battery systems, hybrid integration equipment, and monitoring — within a controlled technology ecosystem. We work with carefully selected global manufacturers and selectively rebrand OEM components to ensure quality control, compatibility, and service continuity. We are not a high-volume reseller; technology selection is deliberate and performance-driven.
