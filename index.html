Hello

We keep the power on. That's the whole job.

Evolta is a Haitian energy company. For more than twenty years, the people behind it have been putting up solar and hybrid systems in some of the toughest conditions around — and keeping them running long after the install crew has gone home.

Power here can be unpredictable. Our job is to make yours boring: steady, affordable, and there when you flip the switch. No surprises, no runaround, and nobody pointing fingers when something needs a look.

How It Works

Three steps. We handle the rest.

No jargon, no army of contractors to chase. From the first chat to year ten, you deal with us.

STEP 01

We talk it through

Tell us what you're powering and what your bills look like. We'll work out what makes sense for you — honestly, even when that's less than you expected to spend.

STEP 02

We design and install it

One team handles the plan, the equipment, and the build. You're not juggling a cast of subcontractors — you've got a single number to call, and it's ours.

STEP 03

We look after it

We keep an eye on your system, handle the upkeep, and answer when you call. For us, the install is the start of the relationship — not the end of it.
