EVOLTA ENERGY

Comprehensive Company Overview & Brand Foundation

1. Company Overview

Evolta Energy is a Haiti-based energy solutions company delivering turnkey solar and hybrid energy systems across residential, commercial, and industrial markets.

The company is built on over 20 years of industry experience and a proven track record of successful energy system installations in Haiti. Evolta’s leadership has been directly involved in the engineering, deployment, and operational management of energy infrastructure in complex and demanding environments.

Evolta was founded with a clear objective: to provide reliable, engineered, and service-driven energy solutions in a market where long-term accountability and execution quality are often inconsistent.

The company is structured intentionally — with strong governance, engineering discipline, and customer service at its core.

2. Market Context

Haiti presents a challenging energy landscape characterized by:

Unstable grid supply

Heavy reliance on diesel generation

Volatile fuel pricing

Limited structured financing options

Inconsistent long-term service standards

Customers are not simply purchasing solar panels.

They are seeking:

Predictable energy costs

Reduced fuel dependency

Operational continuity

Reliable system performance

A trusted long-term partner

Evolta’s deep in-market experience enables the company to design solutions specifically adapted to Haiti’s energy realities.

3. What Evolta Delivers

Evolta’s core business is the delivery of structured, turnkey solar and hybrid energy systems.

Each project includes:

Load and consumption analysis

Financial and performance modeling

Engineering-driven system architecture

Controlled equipment ecosystem (rebranded OEM and selective co-branding with established global manufacturers)

Turnkey installation

Commissioning and system validation

Long-term service, monitoring, and lifecycle support

The company prioritizes lifecycle performance and reliability over short-term installation volume.

4. Selective Component Supply

In addition to turnkey solutions, Evolta will selectively supply key system components such as:
